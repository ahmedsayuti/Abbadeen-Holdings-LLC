Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How quickly can I get a quote?+
We respond to all quote requests within 2 business hours. For urgent or same-day freight needs, please call us directly at +1 (000) 000-0000 — our team is available 24/7 for time-critical enquiries.
What temperature ranges do you handle?+
Our refrigerated fleet covers the full spectrum from +10°C (fresh produce) down to -18°C (deep-frozen goods). Every compartment is temperature-logged continuously and HACCP-certified for food safety compliance.
Where do you operate?+
Abbadeen Holdings, LLC operates nationwide across the United States. We cover major agricultural corridors and have active routes across the Southeast, Midwest, and Northeast. If you're unsure whether we cover your specific route, just ask — we're expanding constantly.
Can I track my shipment in real time?+
Yes — all shipments through Abbadeen are tracked via our Beta Relay 2.0 platform. Depending on your service tier, you'll receive live GPS location updates, ETA notifications, temperature logs, and direct alerts if anything changes in transit.
What is the Beta Relay 2.0 platform?+
Beta Relay 2.0 is our flagship National Over-the-Road Freight Coordination Platform — an app-based operational intelligence system that provides live vehicle dashboard sync, route-to-market optimization, baton-relay driver coordination, and national capacity management. It's what makes Abbadeen different from a traditional freight carrier.
Do you offer same-day or express delivery?+
Yes. We offer same-day pickup and next-morning delivery for qualifying routes and cargo types, including premium produce, seafood, and specialty food items. Contact us with your specific requirements and we'll confirm availability for your corridor.
How does the farm pickup service work?+
Our farm-gate collection service is simple: schedule a pickup time around your harvest window via phone, email, or our platform. Our refrigerated vehicle arrives at your specified location, loads your produce with care, and immediately begins its cold-chain journey to the destination of your choice.
Are you open to new partnerships?+
Absolutely. We actively seek partnerships with farm cooperatives, CPG brands, restaurant groups, hospitality chains, and logistics technology providers. Use the contact form above or reach out directly at info@abbadeenholdings.org — we typically respond within a few hours.

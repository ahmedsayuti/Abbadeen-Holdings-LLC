Beta Relay 2.0 Goes Live — A Game-Changer for U.S. Freight

After months of development, our flagship National Over-the-Road Freight Coordination Platform is officially operational. Beta Relay 2.0 transforms fragmented U.S. trucking into a synchronized logistics infrastructure — combining real-time route-to-market optimization, a novel baton-relay driver network, and live vehicle dashboard sync for administrators.

The platform directly addresses federal priorities: a $875B freight sector demanding efficiency, an 80,000+ driver shortage met by our relay model, public safety improvements through fatigue reduction, and CO₂ emissions cuts via intelligent routing.

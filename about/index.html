Our Story

How Abbadeen
Came to Be

Abbadeen Holdings, LLC was born from a simple but powerful observation: the U.S. food supply chain was broken. Produce sat in transit too long, temperatures fluctuated, and farmers had no reliable technology to bridge the gap between their fields and the restaurant table.

So in 2024, we built the solution — a fully integrated, cold-chain freight company powered by next-generation logistics technology. In just two years we've grown from concept to a nationally operating LLC, and we're only getting started.

  • 2024 — Q1
    Incorporated — Abbadeen Holdings, LLC officially registered in the United States.
  • 2024 — Q3
    First Routes Live — Initial cold-chain runs established across the Southeast corridor.
  • 2024 — Q4
    Farm Partner Network — Onboarded 200+ farm partners across 8 states.
  • 2025
    Beta Relay 2.0 Launched — Our flagship freight intelligence platform goes live nationally.
  • Now — 2025 & Beyond
    National Scale — 850+ farm partners, 2,400+ active routes, growing daily.

Purpose & Direction

Mission, Vision
& Commitment

🎯
Mission
Why We Exist

To connect American farmers directly to tables — eliminating spoilage, reducing supply chain friction, and delivering fresh, cold-chain-certified produce to every restaurant, hotel, and distributor we serve. We exist to make farm-to-table freight reliable, transparent, and fast.

🔭
Vision
Where We're Headed

To become the most trusted name in U.S. farm-to-table freight — a national logistics infrastructure that not only moves food, but transforms how the entire CPG and food-service industry thinks about cold-chain supply chains. Our Beta Relay 2.0 platform is the engine driving that future.

🌿
Commitment
Our Promise

Every load we carry is a promise — to the farmer who grew it, the chef who ordered it, and the diner who will eat it. We commit to temperature integrity, on-time performance, sustainability, and transparent communication every single time.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

The principles behind every route we run, every load we carry, and every relationship we build.

❄️
Cold-Chain Integrity

We never compromise on temperature. Every shipment is monitored, logged, and HACCP-compliant from pickup to final delivery. Fresh arrives fresh — always.

⚡
Speed & Reliability

Built in 2024, we have no legacy systems holding us back. Our technology-first approach means faster response times, smarter routing, and a 98% on-time record that speaks for itself.

🌿
Sustainability

We invest in low-emission vehicles and optimized routing that reduces both our carbon footprint and your supply chain costs. Green freight is good freight.

🤝
Partnership First

We don't just haul — we partner. Every farm, restaurant, and distributor in our network gets a dedicated logistics coordinator who understands their unique needs.

🔬
Innovation

From Beta Relay 2.0's live vehicle dashboard sync to our baton-relay driver network, we use technology to solve problems the industry didn't know could be solved.

📊
Transparency

You always know where your load is, what temperature it's at, and when it will arrive. No surprises, no excuses — just clear, real-time communication at every step.

National Over-the-Road Freight Coordination Platform

An app-based operational intelligence system that transforms fragmented U.S. trucking operations into a synchronized logistics infrastructure for the Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) industry. Integrating real-time route-to-market optimization, a novel baton-relay driver network, and nationwide capacity coordination — Beta Relay 2.0 gives administrators exact live awareness of every vehicle and route at any moment.
